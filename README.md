# Construction-Industry-Company-Finance-Data
This is a dataset from CSMAR, combined with digital transformation, for everyone to conduct empirical analysis

## The meaning of each variable
stock_code	Unique company stock identifier
year	Observation year
industry_code	Industry classification code
digital	Digitalization index measuring the firm’s digital adoption level
capital	1 = capital intense
0 = light capital
tfp_lp	Total Factor Productivity
op_efficiency	Operating efficiency of the firm
roa	Return on Assets — net profit divided by total assets
rndr	R&D expenditure ratio
iapply	Degree of technology application
tat	Total Asset Turnover — revenue generated per unit of total assets
mer	administrative expense ratio
size	logarithm of total assets
top1	Ownership %share of the largest shareholder
growth	Revenue growth rate
slack	Current ratio — liquidity indicator measuring financial flexibility
lev	Leverage ratio — total debt divided by total assets
tier1	Indicator for firms located in first-tier cities (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
state_owned	Indicator for state-owned enterprises (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
